Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Your Privacy, Respected

I don’t want to be tracked. So I won’t track you. This site doesn’t use Google Analytics, Meta pixels, fingerprinting scripts, or any other shady tech that spies on what you’re doing. No ads. No hidden cookies. Just content.

Data Collection

I don’t collect personal data. I don’t run a database behind this site. If you email me, I’ll obviously have your email address — but that’s between you and me. I won’t sell it, trade it, or do anything weird with it.

Infrastructure

This site is protected behind Cloudflare, which may collect basic logs for security and performance — but I don’t access or use that data. I trust them to keep things fast and secure.

Third Parties

I don’t use third-party tracking or embedded services that monitor your activity. No YouTube iframes, no analytics SDKs, no surprises.

Changes

If I ever change this policy, I’ll update it here and keep things just as transparent. I don’t plan on turning evil. But if I do, this will be the first place to show it.
